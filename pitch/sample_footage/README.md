# Sample footage

Pitch does not ship match videos. Add your own files here or open any video from disk.

**Free sources**

- Search [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com) or [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com) for “football aerial” or “soccer match”.
- Use a phone recording of a local match.

**What works best**

- Broadcast or elevated angle (not ground-level only).
- **720p or 1080p**, **25–30 fps** gives smoother tracking than very low resolution.

Place downloaded `.mp4` files in this folder if you like, or use **Open Video** and pick any path.
